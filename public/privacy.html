Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

1. Introduction

ShortsHub ("we," "our," or "the Service") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our web application.

2. Information We Collect

  • YouTube Data: We access publicly available information from the YouTube API, such as channel name, avatar, subscriber count, and Shorts metadata. This data is used to provide the tracking features of the Service.
  • TikTok Account Data: If you authorize us to post to TikTok, we receive your TikTok username, profile picture, and account ID. This information is only used to manage your TikTok posts from ShortsHub.
  • Usage Metrics: We may collect anonymized data about how you interact with ShortsHub to improve the user experience and performance of the Service.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide real-time tracking of YouTube Shorts for the channels you specify.
  • Facilitate the posting of content to TikTok on your behalf.
  • Improve the reliability and features of ShortsHub.
  • Respond to your requests or inquiries.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share your data only with third-party service providers (such as YouTube and TikTok) as necessary for the Service to function as intended.

5. Third-Party Services

ShortsHub uses YouTube API Services. By using our application, you agree to the Google Privacy Policy, which is available at https://www.google.com/policies/privacy.

You can manage your security settings and revoke access via the Google Security Settings page at https://myaccount.google.com/permissions.

Similarly, when connecting to TikTok, you agree to TikTok's privacy policy and terms of use.

6. Data Retention

We retain your authorization tokens and tracking data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Service. You can revoke our access to your accounts at any time through the respective platform settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke ShortsHub's access to your YouTube or TikTok data.
  • Request the deletion of any data we have stored related to your use of the Service.
  • Contact us if you have concerns about your privacy.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of the page. Your continued use of the Service after any such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
